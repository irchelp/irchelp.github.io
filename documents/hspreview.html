Welcome to #irchelp

/I-R-C/ n. [Internet Relay Chat] - IRC provides a way of communicating in real time with people from all over the world. It consists of various separate networks (or "nets") of IRC servers, machines that allow users to connect to IRC.

/#irchelp/ n. - A channel on the EFNet IRC network dedicated to supporting IRC users since 1993.
